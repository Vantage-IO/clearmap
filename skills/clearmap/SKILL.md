# ClearMap (compatibility entry point)

ClearMap now ships two focused skills:

- **clearmap-development** for HIPAA-aware healthcare development: safer PHI handling, minimum-necessary data, access control, audit events, and no PHI in logs or prompts, while still shipping the feature.
- **clearmap-audit** for running the technical-risk audit and reporting the ClearMap HIPAA Technical Risk Score.

Use those directly. This entry remains for existing references. ClearMap is a technical-risk signal, not a HIPAA certification, and finding no issues never means a product is HIPAA compliant.
